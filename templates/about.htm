Image to Text

Text Recognition System

Convert images into documents with “live text” Select your image and upload here. Once you got the preview
go for recognize and you will get 'live text' from the image.

How to use the system?

  • Click on the '+' to select a image and click 'upload' .
  • Once you got preview click 'Recognize'.
  • You will obtain the 'live text' from the image,
    here you can edit the text.Click on 'Save' to save as document.
